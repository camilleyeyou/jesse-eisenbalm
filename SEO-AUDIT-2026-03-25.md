## Prioritized Action Plan

### Critical (Fix Immediately)

1. **Fix sitemap generation** — Debug why regex filtering fails in generate-sitemap.js. Consider switching to proper XML parsing instead of regex. Re-run `npm run prebuild` and verify output contains only 19 blog posts + 5 static pages = 24 URLs total.

2. **Name the charity** — Add the specific charity name, link, and any verification (501(c)(3) number, donation receipts). This is the brand's primary trust differentiator and it's currently unsubstantiated.

### High Priority (Fix This Week)

3. **Add FAQPage schema** to FaqPage.jsx — The 11 Q&A pairs are already perfectly structured. Wrap in `@type: FAQPage` with `mainEntity` containing Question/Answer objects.

4. **Preload hero poster image** — Add `<link rel="preload" as="image" href="/images/hero-poster.jpg" fetchpriority="high" />` to index.html. Expected LCP improvement: 300-800ms.

5. **Fix duplicate H1** — Change the second `<h1>` ("Premium Beeswax Lip Balm") to `<h2>` on the homepage.

6. **Rewrite homepage title tag** — Target real search queries: "Premium Natural Beeswax Lip Balm" instead of "Digital Wellness Ritual for Business Professionals."

7. **Remove meta keywords tag** — Google ignores it since 2009. Its presence signals outdated SEO.

8. **Fix cart quantity button sizes** — Change `w-8 h-8` to `w-12 h-12` (48px minimum for mobile tap targets).

9. **Add preconnect for backend** — `<link rel="preconnect" href="https://jesse-eisenbalm-server.vercel.app" crossorigin />` in index.html.

10. **Add `@id` graph linking** to all schema blocks — Connect Organization, WebSite, Product, and page schemas into a unified knowledge graph.

### Medium Priority (Fix This Month)

11. **Add HSTS header** — `Strict-Transport-Security: max-age=63072000; includeSubDomains; preload` in vercel.json.

12. **Throttle parallax scroll handler** with requestAnimationFrame and consolidate both scroll listeners.

13. **Create a return/refund policy page** — Required for e-commerce trust signals.

14. **Expand the About page** — Add real founder story, product development narrative, sourcing details. Meet 800-word minimum.

15. **Add expert review attribution** on skincare/health claim content, or remove clinical claims.

16. **Fix BlogPosting author type mismatch** — Use Person consistently (pre-render uses Organization, client uses Person).

17. **Add AI crawler directives** to robots.txt (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot).

18. **Defer GA4 inline config** — Move to end of `<body>` or wrap in setTimeout for LCP improvement.

19. **Extract inline CSS** from EisenbalmShop.jsx (~200 lines) to a separate .css file.

20. **Fix product image dimensions** — Change width/height to match aspect-square container (1024x1024 instead of 1024x1536).
